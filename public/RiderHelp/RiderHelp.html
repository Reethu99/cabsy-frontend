Ride Fare Related Issues

How is the ride fare calculated?

Ride fares are calculated based on a combination of factors including distance traveled, time taken, current demand (surge pricing), and any applicable tolls or taxes. You can see an estimated fare before confirming your ride.

Why was I charged more than the estimated fare?

Discrepancies can occur due to changes in the route, extended waiting times, or if the initial estimated fare did not account for all variables. Please check your ride summary. If you believe there's an error, you can report it through the app's 'Help' section for that specific ride.

How do I get a refund for an incorrect charge?

To request a refund, go to your past rides history in the app, select the ride with the issue, and choose 'Report an issue'. Provide details about the incorrect charge. Our support team will review your request and process refunds accordingly.

Captain and Vehicle Related Issues

My captain did not arrive or cancelled the ride. What should I do?

If your captain did not arrive, you can cancel the ride and book another one. If the captain cancelled, you will not be charged. You can also report this issue through the app for us to investigate the captain's behavior.

How can I report a bad experience with a captain?

You can rate your captain after every ride. If you had a particularly bad experience, you can provide detailed feedback and report the issue through the 'Help' section related to that specific ride. Your feedback helps us maintain service quality.

The vehicle was not as described or had issues.

We strive to ensure vehicles meet our standards. If the vehicle was different from what was shown or had safety/maintenance issues, please report this immediately through the app, providing as many details as possible (e.g., license plate, specific issues).

Pass and Payment Related Issues

My payment failed. What should I do?

Check your internet connection and ensure your payment method details are correct. You can try a different payment method or contact your bank/payment provider. If the issue persists, please contact our support team.

How do I add or remove a payment method?

You can manage your payment methods in the 'Payment' section of your app's profile. You can add new credit/debit cards, link digital wallets, or remove existing payment options.

I purchased a pass, but it's not showing up.

Sometimes it takes a few minutes for new passes to reflect in your account. Please try restarting the app. If it still doesn't appear after some time, provide your transaction details to our support team for verification.

Parcel Related Issues

My parcel was damaged during delivery.

We advise users to securely pack parcels. If your parcel was damaged due to mishandling, please take photos and report the incident immediately to our support team with details of the damage and the delivery.

My parcel was lost or delivered to the wrong address.

In case of a lost parcel or incorrect delivery, contact our support team with the parcel tracking ID, sender and receiver details, and any relevant information. We will initiate an investigation and keep you updated.

Can I send fragile or valuable items as parcels?

While our service is available for parcels, we recommend not sending highly fragile, perishable, or extremely valuable items. Please refer to our terms and conditions for a list of prohibited items and guidelines for packing.
