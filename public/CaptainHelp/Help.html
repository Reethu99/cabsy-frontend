Frequently Asked Questions

You can update your profile picture from the "Settings" section of your app. Go to "Profile" and then tap on your current picture to upload a new one.

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" and follow the instructions to reset it using your registered email or phone number.

When a ride request comes in, you will see a notification on your screen. Tap "Accept" to take the ride. Make sure your app is online to receive requests.

If a passenger cancels a ride, you may be eligible for a cancellation fee depending on the time of cancellation and your location. Check your earnings section for details.

Earnings are typically processed daily/weekly, depending on your region and payment method. You can view your payout schedule in the "Earnings" section.

Try clearing the app's cache and data, or reinstalling the app. Ensure your operating system is up to date. If the issue persists, contact support.

In case of an incident, go to the "Help" section and select "Report an Incident" or contact our emergency support line immediately.

You can contact customer support via the in-app chat, phone, or email. Details are available in the "Contact Us" section.
