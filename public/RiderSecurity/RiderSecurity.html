Your Safety is Our Priority

At Cabsy, we're dedicated to building a secure platform for every user. Learn about our safeguards and how you can stay safe.

How Cabsy Keeps You Safe

Data Encryption

Your personal information and payment details are encrypted using industry-leading protocols, both when in transit and when stored.

Rigorous Driver Vetting

Every Cabsy driver undergoes thorough background checks and vehicle inspections to ensure your peace of mind.

Real-time GPS Tracking

All rides are tracked via GPS, allowing you to share your trip status with loved ones for added security.

24/7 Support

Our dedicated support team is available around the clock to assist you with any concerns or emergencies.

Secure Authentication

We implement robust authentication measures like strong password policies and optional multi-factor authentication (MFA).

Continuous Monitoring

Our systems are constantly monitored for suspicious activity, ensuring quick detection and response to potential threats.

Your Role in Staying Secure

While Cabsy builds a secure platform, your active participation is key to your safety.

Strong Passwords

Use unique, complex passwords for your Cabsy account. Consider using a password manager.

Verify Your Ride

Always check the driver's photo, vehicle make/model, and license plate number in the app before getting in the car.

Be Aware of Phishing

Be cautious of suspicious emails or messages asking for personal information. Cabsy will never ask for your password via email.

Share Your Trip

Utilize the "Share My Trip" feature to let friends or family track your journey in real-time.

Report Issues

If you encounter any security concerns or suspicious behavior, report it immediately through the app or to our support team.

Secure Wi-Fi

Avoid accessing sensitive information over unsecured public Wi-Fi networks.
